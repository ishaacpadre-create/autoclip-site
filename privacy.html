Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 3, 2026

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how Autoclip ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, stores, and protects your personal information when you use our content creation tool ("the Service"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and handling your data transparently.

By using Autoclip, you consent to the data practices described in this policy.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide

When you use Autoclip, we may collect the following information:

  • Account authentication data: When you connect your TikTok or YouTube account, we receive an authentication token (OAuth access token) that allows us to post content on your behalf.
  • Contact information: Your email address, if you contact us for support.

2.2 Information Collected Automatically

Autoclip does not automatically collect any personal data. We do not use cookies, web beacons, analytics trackers, or any other automatic data collection technology.

2.3 Information from Third Parties

When you connect your social media accounts, we receive limited profile information from those platforms (such as your username and account ID) solely for the purpose of authenticating and publishing content. We do not access your followers, messages, contacts, or any other social data.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect exclusively for the following purposes:

DataPurpose
OAuth access tokensTo publish video content to your connected social media accounts as directed by you
Account ID / usernameTo identify which account to publish content to
Email addressTo respond to support inquiries

We do not use your data for advertising, profiling, marketing, or any purpose other than providing the Service.

4. Data Storage and Security

All authentication tokens and credentials are stored locally on your device only. Autoclip does not transmit or store your credentials on any external server or cloud service.

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your data, including:

  • Local-only storage of sensitive credentials
  • No remote database or cloud storage of personal data
  • Authentication tokens are only used for their intended purpose

5. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, rent, trade, or otherwise share your personal information with any third parties. Your data is never shared with advertisers, data brokers, or marketing companies.

The only third-party interactions occur when you direct Autoclip to publish content to your connected social media accounts (TikTok, YouTube). In these cases, data is sent directly to those platforms via their official APIs, subject to their own privacy policies.

6. Data Retention

We retain your data only for as long as necessary to provide the Service:

  • Authentication tokens: Stored locally until you disconnect your account or revoke access.
  • Video content: Processed locally and not retained after publishing. You maintain full control of your content files on your device.
  • Support emails: Retained for up to 12 months after your last correspondence.

You can delete all locally stored data at any time by removing the Autoclip application and its associated configuration files from your device.

7. Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Right to access: You can request information about what data we hold about you.
  • Right to deletion: You can request that we delete any personal data we hold about you. Since data is stored locally, you can also delete it yourself at any time.
  • Right to revoke access: You can disconnect your social media accounts at any time, which revokes Autoclip's access to those platforms.
  • Right to data portability: Your data is stored in standard formats on your device and can be accessed at any time.
  • Right to withdraw consent: You can stop using the Service at any time.

8. Children's Privacy

Autoclip is not intended for use by individuals under the age of 13 (or the applicable age of digital consent in your jurisdiction). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe that a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us and we will take steps to delete that information.

9. International Data Transfers

Since all personal data is stored locally on your device, no international data transfers occur through our Service. When you publish content to third-party platforms, those transfers are governed by the respective platform's privacy policies.

10. Third-Party Services

Autoclip integrates with the following third-party services:

  • TikTok: For publishing video content. Subject to TikTok's Privacy Policy.
  • YouTube / Google: For publishing video content. Subject to Google's Privacy Policy.

We recommend reviewing the privacy policies of these third-party services before connecting your accounts.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or for legal, regulatory, or operational reasons. When we make changes, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this page periodically.
